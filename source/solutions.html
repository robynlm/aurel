aurel.solutions

Here are a couple solutions to Einstein’s field equations. Functions in these modules generate numerical data based on analytical expressions.

solutions.Collins_Stewart

This is a Collins and Stewart 1971 solution that describes a Bianchi II γ-law perfect fluid homogeneous solution. See section 3.3 of 2211.08133
